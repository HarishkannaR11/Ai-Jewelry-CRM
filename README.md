# 💎 AI Jewelry ERP (SaaS CRM Platform)

> Multi-tenant SaaS ERP & CRM platform for jewelry businesses with AI-powered workflows, billing automation, and enterprise-grade business management.

---

## 📌 Overview

AI Jewelry ERP is a cloud-based **multi-tenant SaaS platform** designed for jewelry retailers to digitize and manage their entire business workflow.

It provides a unified system for:
- Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Jewelry inventory tracking
- GST billing & invoice generation
- Gold loan management
- Repair & service workflows
- Gold savings schemes
- AI-powered business insights

> 📌 This project was developed as an internship MVP based on real-world ERP requirements for jewelry businesses. It is a portfolio case study and does not include proprietary source code.

---

## 🏢 System Architecture

- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture (isolation per store)
- Modular ERP system design
- REST API-based backend architecture
- AI integration layer using external services (Sarvam AI)
- Event-driven workflows for billing and inventory updates
